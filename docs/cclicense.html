Two Creative Commons license examples

The license at the top of the page was generated on Monday December 9, 2024 from the Creative Commons license chooser. When I created it and embedded on this page, I found that each img element was flagged as an error by oXygen (and there are several errors). Fixing this error was easy: I had to just close each of the self-closing <img/> elements by setting its / in the proper place:

Before: (errors! img elements not closed!)

            <img
                        style="height:22px!important;margin-left:3px;vertical-align:text-bottom;"
                        src="https://mirrors.creativecommons.org/presskit/icons/sa.svg?ref=chooser-v1"
                        alt="">

After: (corrected!)

             <img
                        style="height:22px!important;margin-left:3px;vertical-align:text-bottom;"
                        src="https://mirrors.creativecommons.org/presskit/icons/sa.svg?ref=chooser-v1"
                        alt=""/>
